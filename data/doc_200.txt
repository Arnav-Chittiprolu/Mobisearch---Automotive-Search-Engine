[ 1 ] Bolechowo is the seat of Solaris Bus & Coach , a Polish bus, coach, trolleybus and tram manufacturer. Solaris opened a solar powered Warehouse Hall and Charging Park on 29 September 2022, which will be used to test charging and discharging of e-vehicles . [ 2 ] History [ edit ] Nine Polish citizens were murdered by Nazi Germany in the village during World War II .
