Find sources: "Italian tuneup" – news · newspapers · books · scholar · JSTOR ( October 2019 ) ( Learn how and when to remove this message ) Italian tuneup is a slang automotive term for attempting to restore engine performance by driving a car at high engine speed (rpm) and load. The term originated from Italian mechanics in the 1950s using this practice to burn off carbon deposits from the spark plugs of sports cars.
