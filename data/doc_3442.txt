From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia 2008 video game 2008 video game Car Jack Streets Developer Tag Games Publisher Tag Games Platforms Mobile phone , iOS ( iPhone ), Nintendo DS , PlayStation Portable Release 23 October 2008 Mobile EU : 23 October 2008 iOS 27 April 2009 [ 1 ] DS NA : 15 March 2010 EU : 19 March 2010 PSP NA : 29 April 2010 EU : 14 July 2010 Directors Cut 19 December 2012 Genre Racing Mode Single-player Car Jack Streets is an action-adventure game developed and published by Tag Games for mobile phones in 2008, for iPhone in 2009, and for Nintendo DS and PlayStation Portable in 2010. A Directors Cut edition was published by Tagplay in 2012.
