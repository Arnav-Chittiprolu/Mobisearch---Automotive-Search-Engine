Kevin Czinger (Founder and Executive Chairman) Products Sports cars Number of employees 100+ Parent Divergent Technologies Website www .czinger .com Czinger Vehicles Inc. , commonly known as Czinger is an American automobile manufacturer of hybrid sports cars based in Los Angeles , California , operating since 2019. [ 1 ] History [ edit ] In 2019, Lukas Czinger and Kevin Czinger founded the company. [ 2 ] Czinger Vehicles is headquartered in Los Angeles , and designs and manufactures supercars. [ 3 ] The first vehicle developed and constructed by the company was the 21C . [ 4 ] The 21C entered production in 2025. [ 5 ] The 21C is notable for its performance, and use of generative design and additive manufacturing . [ 6 ] The 21C is planned to be a limited run of 80 vehicles. [ 7 ] Czinger Vehicles is represented by a global network of luxury automotive dealers including O'Gara Coach in California and H.R. Owen in London, UK.
