Do not fill this in! === Vehicle construction ===
GHGs are also emitted when the electric vehicle is being manufactured. The lithium-ion batteries used in the vehicle take more materials and energy to produce because of the extraction process of the lithium and cobalt essential to the battery.<ref>{{Cite news|title=Cleaner Cars from Cradle to Grave (2015)|language=en|work=Union of Concerned Scientists|url=http://www.ucsusa.org/clean-vehicles/electric-vehicles/life-cycle-ev-emissions#.W_CtP-hKjIV.|access-date=3 December 2018}}</ref> This means the bigger the electric vehicle, the more carbon dioxide emitted. The same size-to-emission relationship applies to manufacturing of all products.

==== Terrestrial Mining ====
The mines that are used to produce the lithium and cobalt used in the battery are also creating problems for the environment, as fish are dying up to {{cvt|150|mi|disp=flip}} downstream from mining operations due to chemical leaks and the chemicals also leak into the water sources the people that live near the mines use, creating health problems for the animals and people that live nearby.<ref>{{Cite news|last=Katwala|first=Amit|title=The spiralling environmental cost of our lithium battery addiction|url=https://www.wired.co.uk/article/lithium-batteries-environment-impact|access-date=3 December 2018}}</ref>

==== Deep sea mining ====
{{Main|Deep sea mining}}
