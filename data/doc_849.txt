( December 2022 ) Hengchi Company type Private Traded as Shenzhen Hengchi Automobile Trading Co., Ltd [ 1 ] Industry Automotive Founded 2020 ; 5 years ago ( 2020 ) Founder Xu Jiayin [ 2 ] Headquarters Nansha , China Key people Anders Warming (designer), Selipanov (styling) Website Evergrande Hengchi Auto Hengchi ( Chinese : 恒驰 ), [ 3 ] officially Shenzhen Hengchi Automobile Trading Co., Ltd , was a Chinese automobile manufacturer that specializes in developing electric vehicles owned by Evergrande Group . The subsidiary entered liquidation proceedings in September 2024. [ 4 ] History [ edit ] Evergrande Auto City in Nansha, Guangzhou Hengchi was founded in 2020, and is located in Nansha . There are 9 concept models, all numbered and named Hengchi 1 through Hengchi 9. All vehicles were shown at the Company Event July 2020, [ 5 ] including 4 sedans, 1 MPV, and 4 SUVs. [ 6 ] [ 7 ] The cars were shown at a company event in July 2020. [ 8 ] The investment volume is estimated at 15 billion Yuan (2.14 billion US dollars ). The first and only model produced in series is the Hengchi 5 , a 4.73 meter long SUV. It went on sale in the Chinese home market in July 2022. [ 9 ] In the summer of 2024, the manufacturer had to file for bankruptcy.
