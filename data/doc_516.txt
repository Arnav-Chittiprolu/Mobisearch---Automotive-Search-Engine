From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia HD Hyundai Oilbank Co., Ltd. Native name 에이치디현대오일뱅크 주식회사 Formerly Kukdong Oil Hyundai Oilbank Company type Subsidiary Industry Petroleum Founded November 19, 1964 ; 61 years ago ( 1964-11-19 ) Headquarters Seosan , Chungcheongnam-do , South Korea Parent HD Hyundai Website www.oilbank.co.kr HD Hyundai Oil Bank Co., Ltd. ( Korean : 에이치디현대오일뱅크 주식회사 ) is a petroleum and refinery company with its headquarters in Seosan , South Korea . It was established in 1964 as Kukdong Oil Industry Company ( Korean : 극동정유 ; Hanja : 極東精油 ; RR : Geukdong Jeongyu ; MR : Kŭktongjŏngyu ) and later taken over by the Hyundai Group in 1993. [ 1 ] It is currently a part of the HD Hyundai Group .  Its primary business is petroleum products, similar to the SK Energy , GS Caltex , and S-Oil .
