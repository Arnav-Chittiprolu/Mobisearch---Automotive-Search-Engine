Infobox references Chemical compound 2-(2-Methoxyethoxy)ethanol , also known under trade names Methyl carbitol , is an industrial solvent and is also commonly used as a fuel system icing inhibitor (FSII) in jet fuels . It is a clear, colorless, hygroscopic liquid. Structurally it is an alcohol and an ether , with a formula  CH 3 OCH 2 CH 2 OCH 2 CH 2 OH. At direct contact it causes drying of skin by leaching fats, and is mildly irritating to the eyes. It is flammable.
