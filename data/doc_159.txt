Compressed hydrogen in hydrogen tanks at 350 bar (5,000 psi) and 700 bar (10,000 psi) is used for mobile hydrogen storage in hydrogen vehicles . [ 1 ] It is used as a fuel gas . Infrastructure [ edit ] Compressed hydrogen is used in hydrogen pipeline transport and in compressed hydrogen tube trailer transport.
