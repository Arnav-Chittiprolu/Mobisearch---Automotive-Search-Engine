( 日本ハム株式会社 , Nippon Hamu Kabushiki-gaisha ) (English name before 2014: Nippon Meat Packers, Inc. ) is a food processing conglomerate headquartered in Umeda , Kita-ku , Osaka , Japan. History [ edit ] Founded in 1949, the company is commonly known as Nippon Ham (stylized as Nipponham ). As a multinational corporation , Nippon Ham operates subsidiaries around the world, including China and the United States. In addition to its main business of meat packing and other food processing, the company owns the Hokkaido Nippon-Ham Fighters , a professional baseball team in Japan's Pacific League , and owns part of the J.League soccer team, Cerezo Osaka .
