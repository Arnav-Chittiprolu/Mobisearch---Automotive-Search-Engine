From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia South Korean automotive software company Hyundai AutoEver Corporation Native name 현대오토에버 주식회사 Company type Public Traded as KRX : 307950 Founded 2000 ; 25 years ago ( 2000 ) Headquarters Seoul , South Korea Parent Hyundai Motor Group Website hyundai-autoever .com Hyundai AutoEver Corporation ( Korean : 현대오토에버 주식회사 ), a Hyundai Motor Group affiliate, is a mobility software company headquartered in Seoul , South Korea. Founded in 2000, Hyundai AutoEver specializes in software development and system integration, providing IT services throughout the entire group.
