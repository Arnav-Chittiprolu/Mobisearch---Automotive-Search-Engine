N verify ( what is Y N ?) Infobox references Chemical compound Potassium ethylhexanoate is the organic compound with the formula KO 2 CCH(C 2 H 5 )CH 2 CH 2 CH 2 CH 3 .  It is the potassium salt of 2-ethylhexanoic acid .  It is a colorless solid that is soluble in a variety of organic solvents. [ 1 ] It also forms a monohydrate .
