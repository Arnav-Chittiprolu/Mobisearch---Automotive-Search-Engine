From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia Chinese automobile manufacturer Jiangxi Hanteng Automobile Co., Ltd. Company type Private Industry Automotive Founded 2013 Defunct 2022 Fate bankruptcy [ 1 ] Headquarters Shangrao, Jiangxi , China Products Automobiles Website hantengauto.com A Hanteng X7 Hanteng Autos ( Chinese : 汉腾汽车 ; officially Jiangxi Hanteng Automobile Co., Ltd. ) was a privately owned Chinese automobile manufacturer headquartered in Shangrao, Jiangxi , China. [ 2 ] It is a private automobile manufacturing company that mainly focused on the research and development, manufacture and marketing of traditional fuel-powered vehicles, new energy vehicles, and automotive parts. [ 3 ] History [ edit ] Established in November 2013 and headquartered in the Shangrao Economic and Technological Development Zone in Shangrao, Jiangxi . [ 4 ] November, 2013 – parent company established. December, 2013 – Construction of the Hanteng Autos factory started. June, 2014 – New Energy development started with the launch of the Hanteng BMS project with investments up to 37 hundred million rmb. December, 2015 – Production of the first product, Hanteng X7 has begun. November, 2016 – Hanteng X5 introduced to the Chinese market. September, 2017 – Contract signed with Derways, a Russian auto manufacturer, making Russia the first export market for Hanteng Autos. November, 2017 – Hanteng X7S and Hanteng X7 PHEV launched at the Guangzhou Auto Show. April, 2022 – Hanteng declared bankruptcy.
