( June 2020 ) ( Learn how and when to remove this message ) Porsche-Diesel Super Porsche-Diesel Super is a tractor that was manufactured by Porsche between 1956 and 1963. It is powered by an air-cooled, four-stroke, 2466 cc, three-cylinder diesel.
