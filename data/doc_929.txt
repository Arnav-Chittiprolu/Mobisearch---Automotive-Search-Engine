From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia Chief Financial Officer of Tesla Vaibhav Taneja Born 1977/1978 (age 47–48) [ 1 ] India Education Mata Jai Kaur Public School Delhi University ( BCom ) Occupations Chief financial officer and Chief accounting officer at Tesla Vaibhav Taneja is an Indian-American business executive and accountant who is currently serving as chief financial officer of Tesla, Inc. ,  since 2023. He is also the company's chief accounting officer and one of the directors of Tesla India Motors and Energy Private Limited. Education [ edit ] Taneja was born in India and completed his schooling from Mata Jai Kaur Public School New Delhi in 1996. [ 2 ] Then he completed his undergraduation in Bachelor of Commerce from Delhi University in 1999. He received the qualified chartered accountancy from the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India in 2000. [ 3 ] [ 4 ] Career [ edit ] From July 1999 to March 2016, Taneja worked for PwC in India and the United States, ultimately holding the role of senior manager in assurance. [ 5 ] In 2016, he joined SolarCity as its corporate controller. [ 5 ] In February 2017, Taneja joined Tesla, Inc. when SolarCity was acquired, as its assistant corporate controller. [ 6 ] In May 2018, he was promoted to corporate controller. [ 7 ] In March 2019, Taneja became the chief accounting officer, replacing Dave Morton. [ 6 ] [ 7 ] He was also made the director of Tesla India Motors and Energy Private Limited based in Bangalore in January 2021. [ 3 ] [ 8 ] In August 2023, he succeeded Zach Kirkhorn as the chief financial officer. [ 7 ] Compensation [ edit ] Taneja garnered significant attention when his compensation details were revealed in May 2025. Taneja received $139 million ( ~ ₹11,507,774,000 ) in 2024, which notably exceeded the pay of top tech leaders such as Microsoft 's Satya Nadella ($79.1 million; ~ ₹6,548,668,000 ) and Google 's Sundar Pichai ($10.73 million; ~ ₹888,334,000 ) in 2024. This substantial sum encompasses various forms of compensation beyond direct salary. Taneja's basic salary at Tesla was $400,000 ( ~ ₹33,116,000 ); the remainder consisted of stock options and equity linked to performance metrics.
