From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia Division of BMW BMW M Motorsport (formerly BMW Motorsport ) is the division of BMW responsible for motorsport-related activities, including works-run competition programmes in touring car racing , sports car racing , motorcycle racing . The current organisation is a result of a restructure in April 2021, [ 1 ] bringing together the BMW M high-performance division with the competitive motorsport division. Key personnel include Franciscus van Meel, CEO of BMW M GmbH, and Andreas Roos, Head of BMW M Motorsport.
