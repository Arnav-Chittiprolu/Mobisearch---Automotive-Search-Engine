=== Edition 1 ===
Edition 1 started production in 2022 as a limited edition and the sole model available for the first year. It was available for pre-order by the end of the launch event on October 20, 2020. All Edition 1 units are white with a black roof and feature bronze-colored wheels. === EV<sup>3X</sup> ===
Following the end of production for the Edition 1, GMC introduced the EV3X trim in 2023 as model year 2023 for the Pickup body style, and model year 2024 for the SUV body style. The Pickup version has near identical specifications as the Edition 1 and features three electric motors with an output of 1,000 horsepower, along with an EPA estimated range of 329 miles with the off-road package and 355 miles with the standard 22-inch wheels. The SUV version of the EV3X trim also features three electric motors, outputting 830 horsepower in the SUV configuration, along with EPA estimated range of 298 miles with the off-road package and 314 miles with the standard 22-inch wheels.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Site Maintenance |url=https://news.gmc.com/newsroom.detail.html/Pages/news/us/en/2023/apr/0421-hummer.html |access-date=2024-08-24 |website=news.gmc.com}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |last=Johnson |first=Erik |date=April 19, 2024 |title=GMC Hummer EV 3X First Test: Stylin' and Profilin' |url=https://www.motortrend.com/reviews/2023-gmc-hummer-ev-3x-electric-truck-first-test-review/ |website=Motor Trend}}</ref>

=== EV<sup>2x</sup> ===
Includes all the features of EV<sup>2</sup>; however, it will also come standard with four-wheel steering, Crab Walk, adaptive air suspension with adaptive ride control, and optional Extract mode. This trim will output an estimated {{convert|625|hp|kW PS|abbr=on}} using a dual motor system, will have an estimated range of at least {{convert|300|mi}}, and is expected to be available in Spring of 2023.<ref name=":1">{{cite web|last=Chang|first=Brittany|title=GMC will be releasing 3 more variants of its 'super truck' Hummer EV over the next 4 years â€” see how they compare|url=https://www.businessinsider.com/gmc-hummer-ev-trims-compared-edition-1-ev3x-ev2x-ev2-2020-10|access-date=2020-10-30|website=Business Insider|archive-date=October 31, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201031170208/https://www.businessinsider.com/gmc-hummer-ev-trims-compared-edition-1-ev3x-ev2x-ev2-2020-10|url-status=live}}</ref>

=== EV<sup>2</sup> ===
This was set to be the base Hummer EV trim which was to include Super Cruise(R), HD surround view, digital key, infinity roof, a multi pro tailgate, and rear drop glass among other standard features. The EV<sup>2</sup> was expected to output an estimated {{convert|625|hp|kW PS|abbr=on}} using a dual motor system, with an estimated range of at least {{convert|250|mi}}.
