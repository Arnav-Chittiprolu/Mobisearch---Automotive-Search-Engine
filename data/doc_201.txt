From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia (Redirected from IHS Inc. ) US-based information company Accuris Company type Private Industry Information services Headquarters Denver, Colorado , United States Owner KKR & Co. Website accuristech .com Accuris is an information services provider. History [ edit ] IHS [ edit ] Information Handling Services (IHS) [ 1 ] "was founded in 1959 as Information Handling Services to provide information for aerospace engineers through microfilm databases". [ 2 ] It subsequently grew to incorporate other companies in the information services sector such as Cambridge Energy Research Associates , Global Insight , Jane's Information Group , Prime Publications Limited, and John S. Herold, Inc. [ 3 ] In 2008, IHS acquired Fairplay , a firm that assigns IMO identification numbers for ships, companies and registered owners. [ 4 ] In 2016, Englewood, Colorado -based IHS and London-based Markit merged. [ 5 ] [ 6 ] Jerre Stead was chief executive of the pre-merger IHS Inc. from 2006 to 2013 and from 2015 until the merger with Markit. [ 2 ] Markit [ edit ] Markit was founded in 2003 as Mark-it Partners, a financial data provider for daily credit default swap pricing. [ 7 ] [ 8 ] [ 9 ] The company grew via joint ventures and by acquiring other companies, [ 10 ] [ 11 ] [ 12 ] [ 13 ] merging with IHS in 2016. [ 14 ] [ 15 ] S&P Global [ edit ] On 30 November 2020, S&P Global and IHS Markit released information about a definitive all-stock deal [ clarification needed ] for around $44 billion, a deal which would be the largest of the year globally, according to Dealogic data. [ 16 ] [ 17 ] On September 9, 2021, GlobalData announced its purchase of the Pricing and Reimbursement dataset from IHS Markit. [ 18 ] In December 2021, the IHS Markit announced its intention to sell Base Chemicals to News Corp for a reported $295 million as part of an effort to alleviate concerns about competitive effects. [ 19 ] The purchase by S&P Global was finalized on 28 February 2022. [ 20 ] [ 21 ] [ 22 ] IHS Markit acquisitions [ edit ] 10 October 1996: IHS Group purchased oil exploration company Petroconsultants following the death of its founder and president Harry Wassall in November 1995. [ 23 ] [ 24 ] This acquisition was later followed in January 1998 by the IHS Group acquiring the North American oil exploration company Petroleum Information / Dwights LLC. [ 23 ] 18 September 2008: IHS Inc. agreed to purchase the economics forecasting organization Global Insight for $200 million. [ 25 ] [ 26 ] 10 June 2013: R.L. Polk & Company , a provider of auto-industry data including used-car history company Carfax, for $1.4 billion. [ 27 ] 26 September 2017: automotiveMastermind Inc., a provider of predictive analytics and marketing automation for the automotive industry. [ 28 ] 26 September 2017: Macroeconomic Advisers, an independent research firm forecasting the US economy. [ 29 ] 3 August 2018: Ipreo, software solutions and data provider in global capital markets. [ 30 ] [ 31 ] 10 October 2019: Novation Analytics, a specialist provider of software solutions, data analysis and advisory services. [ 32 ] [ 33 ] 2019: Acquired Informa 's Agriculture Intelligence in exchange for most of IHS Markit's Technology, Media and Telecoms division (including Screen Digest ). [ 34 ] 1 May 2020: Catena Technologies Pte Ltd, a global regulatory trade reporting firm based in Singapore. Accuris [ edit ] In 2023, S&P Global announced that it sold its engineering division to KKR & Co . The business was renamed Accuris.
