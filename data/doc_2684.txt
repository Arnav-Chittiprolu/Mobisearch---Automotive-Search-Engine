Find sources: "Alexander Lodge" – news · newspapers · books · scholar · JSTOR ( April 2013 ) ( Learn how and when to remove this message ) ( Learn how and when to remove this message ) Alexander Marshall Lodge (1881–1938) was an English inventor who did early work and held some patents on the spark plug . He and his brother Francis Brodie Lodge (1880–1967) founded a company, Lodge Brothers, in 1903 – which eventually, following a merger with the Mascot Company in 1913, was renamed Lodge Plugs Ltd ; it was based in Rugby , Warwickshire . Much of their work was developed from research by their father, Sir Oliver Lodge . Alec Lodge died on 17 February 1938, aged 56.
