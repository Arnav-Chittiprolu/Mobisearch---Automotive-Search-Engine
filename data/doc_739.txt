From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia Motor vehicle Volvo EX60 Overview Manufacturer Volvo Cars Production 2026 (to commence) Assembly Sweden: Torslanda ( Torslandaverken ) Body and chassis Class Compact luxury crossover SUV Body style 5-door SUV Platform Volvo SPA3 Related Polestar 7 The Volvo EX60 is an upcoming battery electric compact luxury crossover SUV manufactured and marketed by Swedish automaker Volvo Cars . According to Volvo, it is set to officially debut on 21 January 2026. [ 1 ] It is the first vehicle and the first Volvo vehicle to sit on the SPA3 platform .
