{{As of|2011|04}}, [[Romania]] offers a government grant of up to 25% of the price (up to a maximum of {{euro|5,000}}) for the purchase of a new electric car.<ref name=Romania>{{cite web|url=http://www.mediafax.ro/english/romanian-clunkers-program-available-for-electric-car-buyers-starting-next-week-8157168|title=Romanian Clunkers Program Available For Electric Car Buyers Starting Next Week|publisher=MediaFax|date=2011-04-14|access-date=2011-04-14}}</ref> Furthermore, through the cash-for-clunkers program ([[scrappage program]]), those who wish to purchase an electric car will receive vouchers of over â‚¬5,000 total in return for their used car.<ref name=Romania/> For hybrid vehicles, with or without plug-in capabilities, a â‚¬550 grant is offered, plus an additional â‚¬160 grant for hybrid vehicles emitting under 100g/km of {{CO2}}. Combined with the cash-for-clunkers program, the total grant is up to â‚¬2200.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.promotor.ro/masini-noi/dosar-analize/analiza-merita-sau-nu-sa-cumperi-un-hibrid-plug-in-sau-o-masina-electrica-14204615|title=ANALIZÄ‚: meritÄƒ sau nu sÄƒ cumperi un hibrid plug-in sau o maÅŸinÄƒ electricÄƒ?|author=Apropo Media|work=promotor.ro|date=4 May 2015}}</ref>
Electric and hybrid vehicles are exempt from the environmental tax, which also acts as a registration tax.
From March 2015, electric vehicles are also exempt from the annual tax, while hybrid vehicles have a 95% reduction.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.digi24.ro/stiri/sci-tech/auto/impozit-mic-pentru-masinile-electrice-si-hibrid-445904|title=Impozit mic pentru maÈ™inile electrice È™i hibrid|website=www.digi24.ro|date=8 October 2015 }}</ref>

In 2016, the "Rabla Plus" program offered a government grant of {{currency|5,000|EUR}} for the purchase of a new electric car.<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.mmediu.ro/app/webroot/uploads/files/2016-06-14_INFOGRAFIC_Rabla_2016.pdf |title=Infografic rabla 2016 |publisher=Ministry of Environment, Water and Forests |location=Romania |language=ro |date=2016-06-14 |access-date=2016-09-16}}</ref> In 2017, the "Rabla Plus" grant program increased to EUR 10,000 for the purchase of a pure electric vehicle (BEV).
