From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia Motor vehicle Kia Retona Overview Manufacturer Kia Motors Also called Kia Retona Cruiser (KDM Facelift) Production 1998–2003 Assembly South Korea: Gwangju ( Gwangju Plant ) Body and chassis Class Mini SUV ( J ) Body style 3-door SUV Layout Front-engine, four-wheel-drive Chassis Body-on-frame Related Kia KM131 Powertrain Engine 2.0 L FE-DOHC I4 (Mazda) 2.0 L RT turbodiesel I4 Dimensions Length 1998–2000: 4,000 mm (157.5 in) 2000–2003: 4,025 mm (158.5 in) Width 1,745 mm (68.7 in) Height 1,835 mm (72.2 in) Curb weight 1,510 kg (3,329 lb) Chronology Predecessor Asia Rocsta Successor Kia Sportage The Kia Retona is a small mini SUV based on Kia's military jeeps . The name is a portmanteau of return to nature . [ 1 ] Developed by Asia Motors , the car was initially sold as the "Asia Retona" in some markets. The Retona had been using the same platform as the first generation Kia Sportage Convertible. Its competitors included the Lada Niva , Suzuki Jimny and the SsangYong Korando .
