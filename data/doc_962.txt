From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia Automotive nameplate by Nissan The Nissan Latio ( Japanese : 日産・ラティオ , Hepburn : Nissan Ratio ) is an automobile nameplate used by the Japanese automobile manufacturer Nissan between 2004 and 2016 for two small car models: Nissan Latio/Tiida Latio , a subcompact/compact car sold in Southeast Asia and Japan from 2004 to 2012 Nissan Latio, a rebadged Nissan Almera sold in Japan as a captive import from 2012 to 2016 2004–2012 Nissan Tiida Latio sedan (Japan) 2004–2012 Nissan Latio hatchback (Malaysia) 2012–2016 Nissan Latio (Japan) Index of articles associated with the same name This set index article includes a list of related items that share the same name (or similar names).
